Italian Restaurant

Our Story



In the heart of Kakinada, where cobbled streets and aromatic alleyways intertwine, there exists a haven for those seeking an authentic taste of Italy. Welcome to "PATO RESTUARANT," a culinary sanctuary that tells a story of passion, tradition, and the artistry of Italian gastronomy.a dream to bring the warmth of Italian hospitality and the richness of its diverse flavors to a discerning audience. The founders, inspired by their travels through Italy, envisioned a place where every dish would echo the authenticity of its region.
